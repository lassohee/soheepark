What is your favorite season? 'Four seasons knit coaster' is a four-piece coaster that contains graphics about the happiness of the four seasons. It is made with knit coasters to enjoy small seasonal happiness, such as flower viewing in spring, eating cool watermelons in summer, harvesting fresh red apples in autumn, and making snowmen in winter. The blue label tag attached to the tea coaster is a design point and is made in a loop shape, making it easy to hang and dry anywhere. Feel your favorite season on the table at any time.

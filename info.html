SOHEE PARK
INFO
Sohee Park studied furniture design and visual design at Hongik University College of Art. She handles various visual objects using various media, moving between flat and three-dimensional objects, and designs them with inspiration from what she collects through daily observations. She currently works as a brand designer in Seoul and also runs PushPress, a publisher that produces various visual objects related to body movements and exercises.
